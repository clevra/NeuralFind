# Privacy Policy

**Neural Find - Local AI Search** operates 100% locally on the user's device. 

The extension reads text on the active tab solely for the purpose of executing the AI assisted search function. No page text, search prompts, or user data are ever transmitted to any external servers, nor are they collected or stored by the developer.

All AI model inference is performed entirely offline within your browser using WebAssembly and WebGPU hardware acceleration.
